TIM 172B · Winter 2024 Supply Chain Forecasting Excel

Supply Chain Strategy & Demand Forecasting — SPC Plastics

Acted as supply chain consultant for SPC, a recyclable container manufacturer. Designed end-to-end SC strategy from competitive analysis through inventory policy and transportation selection.

  • Applied Porter's Six Forces to determine cost leadership strategy; mapped SPC on IDU and responsiveness/efficiency spectrums to identify strategic fit
  • Evaluated 5 demand forecasting methods in Excel — Static, Moving Average, Simple & Double Exponential Smoothing, Holt's, Winter's — selecting Static based on lowest MAPE (7.66 vs. 64K–773K for alternatives)
  • Built EOQ model: optimal lot size 2,018 units, 13 shipments/year, 29-day replenishment cycle, $3,784 annual holding cost; modeled a 44,921-unit forward-buy to maximize savings on a 20% supplier discount
  • Developed safety inventory policy (6,139 units) using CSL-based modeling; designed supplier scorecard using cost minimization + weighted utility function; selected rail over truck via full annual cost model saving $26,570/year
